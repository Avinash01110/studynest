At University Living, we guide and assist students like you who are in search of homes. With us, you can find suitable and secure accommodation near university campuses. What do we offer?

We offer student accommodation near universities worldwide. Whether you are seeking students living in the United Kingdom, Canada, United States, Australia, Ireland, Europe, etc., you will find many options of student housing that are low-cost to meet your student budget.

Why choose private student accommodation?

Because these student homes have been designed keeping in mind your student needs and preference by reliable and trusted student accommodation providers. With the hassle of finding rooms at university halls, the PBSAs have simplified the lives of the students finding a student home near universities. Private student home is easy to search, find and even get even at the last minute of booking.

What are the types of student accommodation we provide?

Student Housing - This is a specially designed furnished student residence. You can get student studios, suites and ensuite rooms. These student housing options are close to your university. They are fully- furnished and equipped with modern amenities like a gym, games, cinema, etc,. The student rent will also include your utility bills, dedicated maintenance & 24x7 Security.
Private Apartments - These are residential student apartments near universities that have a variety of floor plans. They offer flexible occupancy, complete privacy, personal space and top- class facilities. The student rent will also include your utility bills, on-site maintenance & 24x7 Security.
Homestays - This is a type of accommodation that involves living with the local hosts. It differs from furnished student housing as it offers local cultural experience. That’s not it, it's more affordable options, providing personalized and cozy homely ambience.
University Halls - This is an on-campus student accommodation located within the campus or close to the campus buildings. It is owned by the university.
